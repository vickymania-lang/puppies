Cava-Tzu Breed Overview

Cava-Tzus, known for their endearing features and gentle personality, are cherished for their friendly and adaptable nature. A crossbreed between Cavalier King Charles Spaniels and Shih Tzus, they have gained popularity for their charming appearance and affectionate disposition.

Origins of the Cava-Tzu

The Cava-Tzu’s roots trace back to the intentional breeding of Cavalier King Charles Spaniels and Shih Tzus, aiming to combine the best traits of both breeds. This crossbreed reflects a blend of charm, intelligence, and a loving disposition, contributing to their role as affectionate and cherished family pets.
