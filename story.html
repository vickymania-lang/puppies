Petscares Puppies Online opened their doors in Georgia in 2017, and we have been breeding dogs since 2004. Will began working with us in 2021 and quickly became a favorite with our customers because of his great ability to connect with customers and help them find the perfect puppy! Welcome to our exclusive puppy haven! We take immense pride in connecting families with their newest furry members. Our unique approach ensures quality time with our dogs, allowing you to bond and ask any questions you may have. Unlike traditional pet stores, our boutique-style, appointment-only setting provides a relaxed, pressure-free experience. Matching puppies with the perfect family is our mission. We believe in finding the ideal fit based on personality, temperament, and breed preferences. Like family, we give each pup a special name, and we’re familiar with their likes, dislikes, and unique personalities. Witness the joy when they meet their forever family! Our puppies thrive on play, socialization, and affection. We handle grooming in-house, ensuring they’re in peak health, happy, and all their needs are met daily. Working closely with our trusted local vet, every puppy for sale undergoes a thorough health check before going home. They’re up-to-date on vaccinations and deworming. You’ll receive a Georgia Health Certificate and a one-year health warranty for added peace of mind. Have questions about our puppies for sale? Reach out anytime! With extensive breed knowledge, we’re here to assist in finding the perfect match for your family’s lifestyle. Whether it’s allergies, energy levels, temperament, or compatibility with kids or other pets, we’ve got you covered. Our support doesn’t end after adoption. We’re here to guide you through puppyhood, from potty training tips to product recommendations. We offer all the supplies you’ll need, so you can go straight home and start creating memories with your new fur baby. At Georgia Puppies Online, you’re not just getting a puppy, you’re gaining a dedicated team. Your concerns, big or small, are our priority. Even years down the line, don’t hesitate to reach out. We’re here for you. While our facilities are in Georgia, we offer puppy delivery to neighboring states and across the US and Canada. Contact us for delivery rates. If you prefer, you’re welcome to schedule an appointment to meet and pick up your chosen puppy. We’re committed to providing the healthiest, most playful puppies for sale. This is who we are, and this is how we run our business. Feel free to call or email with any questions or concerns. We’re here for you every step of the way. Geo
